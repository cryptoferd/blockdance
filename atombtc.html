What this is

This renders a stylized atom where the “chemistry” is driven by a Bitcoin block. Surfaces are a seamless metallic shader with vibrant marbling—no UV seams.

How to use

  • Enter a height and press Load, or hit Latest.
  • Rotate = drag · Zoom = scroll/pinch · Pan = right-drag / 3-finger (touch).
  • Sliders: Metalness (left = paint, right = chrome) and Roughness/Grain (left = soft, right = sharp).
